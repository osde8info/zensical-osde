The [Sun Startup Essentials](http://www.sun.com/emrkt/startupessentials/index.jsp) program is specifically designed to help startups get their business off the ground rapidly, and at lower cost. With the Sun Startup Essentials program, your business can get the industry-leading systems it needs at significantly discounted prices.

_Discounted partner hosting services, free software and free technical advice via email are all included._

_In order to qualify for the Sun Startup Essentials program, your U.S. company must meet [criteria](http://www.sun.com/emrkt/startupessentials/about.jsp) including the following:_

- _Be in business for 4 years or less_
- _Have a maximum of 150 employees, including any affiliates_
- _Be based in the United States_
- _Must have a verifiable company presence (website, company profile, etc.)_
- _Agree to accept the program's terms and conditions, the [Startup Essentials Program Enrollment Agreement](http://www.sun.com/emrkt/startupessentials/agreement.pdf)_
- _A valid street address must be provided._
