Yahoo [ZoneTag](http://zonetag.research.yahoo.com/index.php) allows you to upload photos to flickr with ZoneTag geo-tags you can then search for them by location with Yahoo [Zurfer](http://zurfer.research.yahoo.com/).

PS You'll need a Symbian S60 2nd or 3rd edition phone !
