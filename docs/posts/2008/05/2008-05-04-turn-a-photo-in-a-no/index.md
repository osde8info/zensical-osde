What you need :

- A Photo
- A image manipulation Program like [IrfanView](http://www.irfanview.com/) that lets you control size, position and margins of printouts
- A Printer  
    
- A sheet of A4 paper  
    

Steps :

1. Open photo with your chosen program
2. Change print size to 10cm (keep ratio)
3. Chose Landscape Paper  
    
4. Add a horizontal margin / offset of 17cm
5. Add a Vertical margin / offset of 12 cm
6. Print
7. Fold lengthwise
8. Fold widthwise
