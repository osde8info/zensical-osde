[Google Apps](http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/var_1c.html) BETA provides free of charge Email & Web hosting for your domain.

For example if your domain was micromanage.co.uk Google Apps BETA could provide the following free of charge services:

Google Hosted Web Pages  
http://www.micromanage.co.uk

Google Hosted Web Pages Configuration  
http://www.micromanage.co.uk-a.googlepages.com

Google Hosted Email / WebMail ( 25GB / user )  
http://mail.google.com/a/micromanage.co.uk

Google Chat  
Users can sign in by downloading Google Talk

Google Calendar  
http://www.google.com/calendar/a/micromanage.co.uk

Google Docs  
http://docs.google.com/a/micromanage.co.uk

You can even [create Google App user accounts in bulk !](https://www.google.com/a/cpanel/micromanage.co.uk/Bulk)

What would your internal IT dept charge to provide all these services ?
