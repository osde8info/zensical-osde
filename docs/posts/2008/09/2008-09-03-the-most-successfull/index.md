... and it wasn't even a flickr photowalk ! ... it was The 18th [CloudMade](http://cloudmade.com/) [OpenStreetMap](http://www.openstreetmap.org/user/osde8info) [Summer 2008 Mapping Party](http://upcoming.yahoo.com/event/1068861) ! CloudMade were giving out some great OpenStreetMap [MOO Stickers](http://www.moo.com/products/stickers.php) and I legitimately added photos from the 1 hr photo / geotagging / mapping walk to ALL the flickr groups below.

Interestingly only 6 people had signed up on the [upcoming event](http://upcoming.yahoo.com/event/1068861/)Â  but 16 turned up on the night !

If you want to help change the (map of the) world (or just get a sticker) turn up at the next [OpenStreetMap event](http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/index.php/Current_events) apparently thet meet every week in London !
