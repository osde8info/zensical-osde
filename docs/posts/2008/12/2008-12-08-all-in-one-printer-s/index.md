Are you looking for an All-In-One Printer Scanner for GNU/Linux then look no further than than the HP Photosmart 4500 series (C4580) or HP OfficeJet 7400 series (L7480) !

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/photosmart/photosmart\_c4500\_series.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/photosmart/photosmart_c4500_series.html)

[![hp officejet and hp photosmart all-in-one](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/6a00d4141b9517685e01098153cf4b000d.jpg?w=300)](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/6a00d4141b9517685e01098153cf4b000d.jpg "hp officejet and hp photosmart all-in-one")[![hp officejet and hp photosmart all-in-one](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/6a00d4141b9517685e010980beef8d000b.jpg?w=300)](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/6a00d4141b9517685e010980beef8d000b.jpg "hp officejet and hp photosmart all-in-one")

  

The HP C4500 uses the following inks :  
  

- 350, 350XL black
- 351, 351XL tri-colour
- 348 photo shades  
    

and can be connected via USB or the following Wi-Fi network ports :  
  

- 137, 138, 161
- 427
- 9100, 9220, 9500
