Had a fantastic day yesterday at the [Adobe OnAir AIR 2008 Tour](http://onair.adobe.com/) in London.

Adobe AIR brings Rich Internet Applications to the desktop of all three OS's ! You can use [AIR](http://get.adobe.com/air/) to turn HTML file(s) and JavaScript file(s) into an executable for ANY platform - its a bit like "JVM + FileSystem + SQLite" !

[AIR](http://get.adobe.com/air/) uses WebKit and is therefore compatible with your favorite JavaScript framework and even Google Gears !

Install the runtime, and check out the newest applications.

- [Install Adobe AIR](http://www.adobe.com/go/getair)
- [Download Adobe AIR SDK](http://www.adobe.com/products/air/tools/sdk/)
- [Install Showcase Applications](http://www.adobe.com/go/airshowcase)

  
[AIR](http://get.adobe.com/air/) for GNU/Linux ALPHA
