Actually there are more similarities than differences ! Symbian S60, Wi-Fi, VOIP, camera, video,15pin accessory connector etc etc

E61i plus's  

- qwerty keyboard
- 50% bigger screen  
    

  
E65 plus's  

- narrower
- sort of locks when you slide is closed
- unlocks when you slide it open
