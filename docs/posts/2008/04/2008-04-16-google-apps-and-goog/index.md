If you already have a [Google Apps](http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/index.html) account you can add a [Google App Engine](http://google.com/support/a/bin/answer.py?answer=91077) to it !

1. Login into your Google Apps account
2. Goto your Dashboard | Service settings | Add more services
3. Other Services | App Engine Preview
4. Enter App Id
5. Click Add It Now
