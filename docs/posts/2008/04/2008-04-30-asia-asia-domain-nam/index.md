ASIA.ASIA [domain name competition](http://hotornot.domains.asia/contest_form.html)

To win, a contestant must _correctly identify the .Asia domain name that received the highest winning price in the .Asia Sunrise and Landrush auctions, AND be able to provide an estimation of the auction winning price that is closest to the actual result._
