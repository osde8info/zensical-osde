[RedBubble](http://www.redbubble.com/) is a business media network and online art gallery that helps you sell your artwork & photos.

_You can use RedBubble to:  
_

- _[Sell your work](http://www.redbubble.com/info/selling) online;_
- _Connect with a [vibrant community](http://www.redbubble.com/people) of creative folk; or_
- _Buy [framed prints](http://www.redbubble.com/buy-wall-art), [posters](http://www.redbubble.com/buy-posters), [canvas prints](http://www.redbubble.com/buy-canvas-prints), [t-shirts](http://www.redbubble.com/buy-t-shirts), [greeting cards](http://www.redbubble.com/buy-greeting-cards) or your very own [calender](http://www.redbubble.com/groups/redbubble/forums/2/topics/6026-howto-make-calendars)._
