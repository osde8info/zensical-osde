There are multiple editions of [Google Apps](http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/admins/editions_spe.html) and [Google Sites](http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/admins/editions_spe.html) to meet the needs of different organizations.

_So whether you're a small business, a Fortune 500 company, a non-profit or a school, Google can power up your organization with the right services and support.

- New! IMAP for Gmail
- New! Google App Engine Development Platform Preview
