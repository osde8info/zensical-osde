[three.co.uk](http://three.co.uk/personal/index.omp) have just lauched [PAYG 3G broadband](https://my3.three.co.uk/myaccount/mobilebroadbandcosts.done) access

First decide how much bandwidth you need  
(PS I've just been surfing for 1hr and used 20M (upload & download) of my 1G limit)

Procedures:  

1. [Buy a voucher](http://three.co.uk/personal/products_services_/mobile_broadband_/detail.omp)  
    
2. Enter and [activate voucher code](https://my3.three.co.uk/myaccount/activateVoucher.done)
3. Use USB dongle (£50) or phone modem (from £12/m) to connect and use  
    

Prices:  

- 1G bandwidth £10 (expires after 30 days)  
    
- 3G bandwidth £15 (expires after 30 days)
