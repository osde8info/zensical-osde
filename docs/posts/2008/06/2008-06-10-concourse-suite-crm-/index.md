[ConcourseSuite](http://www.concursive.com/concoursesuite.shtml) 5.0 is _the first front office application suite to integrate Customer Relationship Management (CRM), web content management and team collaboration capabilities into a single, easy to user web application._

_An open source Java-based application with a standards-based plug-in architecture, [ConcourseSuite](http://www.concursive.com/concoursesuite.shtml) 5.0 can scale to the largest enterprise, yet delivers the ease of use and low cost required by small and medium-sized businesses.  
_  
With a [FREE of charge 100 user](http://www.concursive.com/buydownload.shtml) online edition and an [open source community](http://www.concursive.com/community.shtml) what more could you want ?
