[LINKBOX](http://www.linkbox.eu/index.php?link=2&lang=en) QR codes  

  

_You have questions about a point of interest, an exhibition or a historical building? You would like to receive current timetable information quickly? You would like to reserve tickets for a concert? Using the LINKBOX provides direct on-the-go access to all relevant information without having to type in internet links on your cell phone keypad. Plus: always get up-to-date information; be it changes to timetables and delays or availability of tickets in real time. And because you can easily bookmark the link in your mobile phone you can always access it later to show your family and friends or even send it to them through your phone._
