[Semapedia](http://semapedia.org/community)'s goal is to let everyone collaboratively physically hyperlink their world, thus sharing knowledge and making it accessible to others in a helpful and meaningful way.

[Semapedia](http://semapedia.org/community) believe in bringing knowledge to places and things that matter to others is a great way to help others understand our beautiful and complex world.

[Semapedia](http://semapedia.org/community) invite you _to create and distribute Semapedia-Tags which are in fact cellphone-readable physical hyperlinks to the free online encyclopedia [Wikipedia](http://www.wikipedia.org/) (or any of Wikipedias' sisterprojects such as [Wikibooks](http://www.wikibooks.org/), [Wikinews](http://www.wikinews.org/)[Wikinews](http://www.wikinews.org/) )_
