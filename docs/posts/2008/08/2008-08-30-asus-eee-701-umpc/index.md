On an [ASUS EEE 701 UMPC](http://wiki.eeeuser.com/eee_pc_701) with SSD it takes just a couple of clicks to :

[![asus eee with 3g](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/6a00d4141b9517685e00fa9678cf7d0003.jpg?w=300 "asus eee with 3g")](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/6a00d4141b9517685e00fa9678cf7d0003.jpg)

[asus eee with 3g](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/6a00d4141b9517685e00fa9678cf7d0003.jpg "asus eee with 3g")

  

- update BIOS [http://support.asus.com/download/download.aspx?SLanguage=en-us](http://support.asus.com/download/download.aspx?SLanguage=en-us)  
    
- update Internet Radio [http://www.mediayou.net/web/index.asp?lan=eng](http://www.mediayou.net/web/index.asp?lan=eng)  
    
- upgrade Skype to Video Skype
- install Digital TV DVBT
- makes videos from the webcam [http://www.unicap-imaging.org/](http://www.unicap-imaging.org/)  
    

A PC that's "as easy to use as an iPod" that'd be the ASUS EEE !

This is the way ALL PCs should be ! You can even [hold F9 during startup](http://wiki.eeeuser.com/explain:f9_restore) and you can restore the ENTIRE operating system and all applications to factory default !
