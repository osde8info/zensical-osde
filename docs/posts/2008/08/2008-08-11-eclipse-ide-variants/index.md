As well as the (IBM) Eclipse IDE I especially like [Aptana Studio](http://aptana.com/studio) and [Easy Eclipse](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/home/index.html) variants.

Aptana because of its video tutorials available at [aptana.tv](http://aptana.tv/) and [Easy Eclipse](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/home/index.html) because of its extensive range of pre-packaged add-ons, plug-ins & extensions :

DB  

- [Quantum](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/quantum.html)

PHP  

- [PHP](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/phpeclipse.html)
- [SimpleTest](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/simpletest.html)

SCM & Version Control  

- [SubClipse](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/subclipse.html)
- [MyLyn](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/eclipse-mylyn.html)
- [SubClipse MyLyn](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/subclipse-mylyn.html)

Markup & XML  

- [OrangeVolt](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/eclipsexslt.html)
- [TeXlipse](http://www.easyeclipse.org/site/plugins/texlipse.html)
