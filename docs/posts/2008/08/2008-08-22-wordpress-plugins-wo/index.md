Here are some WordPress plugins worth trying:

- [Google Analytics for WordPress](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/google-analytics-for-wordpress/)
- [Adsense Manager](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/adsense-manager/)
- [Sociable](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/sociable/) adds links to del.icio.us etc on every post (see Share and Enjoy squares below) !  
    
- [XML Google Maps](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/xml-google-maps/)
