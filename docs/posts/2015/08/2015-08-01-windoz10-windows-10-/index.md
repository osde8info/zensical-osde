windows 10 home, pro, k and kn compared

http://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/windows/windows-10-editions

- home = base level windows
- pro = home + domains + remote desktop + hyper v + etc
- KN, N = home or pro BUT without media and video apps and without skype

_“N” and “KN” editions of Windows 10 Home and Windows 10 Pro are also available and include the same functionality as the standard editions, except that they do not include certain media related technologies (Windows Media Player, Camera, Music, Films & TV) and does not include the Skype app._
