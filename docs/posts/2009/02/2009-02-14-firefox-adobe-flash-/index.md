Just noticed that Firefox 3.0.6 on Ubuntu 8.04 gives me a choice of flash player plugins:

- Adobe Flash
- [GNU Gnash](http://www.gnu.org/software/gnash/)

  
Unfortunately Gnash doesn't seem to work with many sites such as flickr or even flash !
