**[Sun xVM VirtualBox](http://www.sun.com/software/products/virtualbox/index.jsp)** downloads are now available for most OS's including GNU/Linux, Mac OSX, Solaris and Windoz !  

- [http://dlc.sun.com/virtualbox/vboxdownload.html](http://dlc.sun.com/virtualbox/vboxdownload.html)

  
_VirtualBox binaries are available for [download](http://www.sun.com/software/products/virtualbox/get.jsp#bin) under the PUEL (Personal Use and Evaluation License). This allows anyone to download and use the product free of charge in compliance with those terms.

_

_For organizations that want to deploy across the business, Sun offers [Sun xVM VirtualBox Enterprise Subscriptions](http://www.sun.com/software/products/virtualbox/get.jsp#sub)  
which includes a Sun Software License Agreement and Premium Service Plan._

**Sun xVM OpenVirtualBox**  
_VirtualBox source code is [freely available](http://www.sun.com/software/products/virtualbox/get.jsp#src) and licensed under GPL2 as the Open Source Edition.
