[OSGeo](http://wiki.osgeo.org/wiki/Public_Geospatial_Data_Project)'s mission is to:

- _Promote the use of open geospatial formats_
- _Provide best-practise guidelines and examples for use of open and free standards for data (GML, WMS, WFS-T) and metadata (Dublin Core, RDF, ISO19115 through ISO19139)._
- _Promote public access to state-collected geodata_
- _Lead by example in demonstrating economic value and research activity generated by open access to public geographic information._
- _Run a repository of open geodata_
