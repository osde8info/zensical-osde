**pins with more information**

http://blog.pinterest.com/post/50883178638/introducing-more-useful-pins

_To start off, we worked with some popular websites to show more information on some pins, so you can do more of what you love. You might see:_

_\-**[Product pins](http://pinterest.com/pinterest/products-we-love/)** for things like clothes and furniture with pricing, availability, and where to buy (updated every day!)_

_\-**[Recipe pins](http://pinterest.com/pinterest/delicious-eats/)** from your favorite bloggers and websites that include cook time, ingredients, and servings_

_\-**[Movie pins](http://pinterest.com/pinterest/lights-camera-action/)** with content ratings, cast members, and more_
