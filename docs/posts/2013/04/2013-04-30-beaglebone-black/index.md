The BeagleBone Black _is the newest member of the BeagleBoard family. It is a lower-cost, high-expansion focused BeagleBoard using a low cost Sitara™ AM3358 [ARM® Cortex™-A8](http://www.element14.com/community/docs/DOC-46525?ICID=knode-beagleboneblack-space) processor from [Texas Instruments](http://www.element14.com/community/community/manufacturers/ti?ICID=knode-beagleboneblack-space), and can connect with the Internet and run software such as Angstrom and Ubuntu. Like its predecessors, the BeagleBone Black is designed to address the Open Source Community, early adopters, and anyone interested in a low cost ARM® Cortex™-A8 based processor._
