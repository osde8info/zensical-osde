a big thanks to kevin, krisha, [samsung mobile](http://www.samsung.com/uk/ "samsung") and three mobile for yesterdays samsung android galaxy workshop where they gave demos of and let us play with a variety of galaxy mobiles and tablets !

we heard about

- using camera while writing SMS's

and

- samsung readers hub (aldiko and kobo based)
- [samsung](http://www.samsung.com/uk/ "samsung") social hub (bmn & smn)
- samsung movie and video hub

and

- samsung allshare multi-media over wi-fi
- samsung google docs integration
- [samsung](http://www.samsung.com/uk/ "samsung") swype keyboard

and the galaxy s camera features

- smile detector
- 360 panorama auto stitcher

and even some tips on using the [samsung](http://www.samsung.com/uk/ "samsung") galaxy portal gt-i5700 like

- using the latest kies phone suite and usb driver
- and you can add the power control widget
