University of Oslo have created [TITAN](https://wiki.uio.no/usit/suf/vd/hpc/index.php/TITAN "uio.no") a HPC composed of 650+ computers having over 5000 cores (CPUs)  running CentOS and is currently one of the Top 10  [Rock Clusters](http://www.rocksclusters.org/rocks-register/index.php?sortby=FLOPS&sortorder=down) in the world !
