[RedGage](http://www.redgage.com/?refby=osde8info "redgage") is a web site that _**pays** people for their online content and monetizes their social activity !_

_It's a centralized hub where you can earn money for all the things you're already doing online at places like Blogger, Flickr, Twitter, YouTube and WordPress ! [RedGage](http://www.redgage.com/?refby=osde8info "redgage") pays based on the value of your content, measured by page views/popularity._

You can connect or link  [RedGage](http://www.redgage.com/?refby=osde8info "redgage") to any existing [blogger](http://osde8info.blogspot.com/ "blogspot"),  [flickr](http://www.flickr.com/photos/osde-info/ "flickr") and [WordPress](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/ "wordpress") accounts in just a few clicks !

_It aggregates from other sites so **you don't even have to regularly visit [RedGage](http://www.redgage.com/?refby=osde8info "redgage")** !_
