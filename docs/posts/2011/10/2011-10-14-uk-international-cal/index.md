If you have a UK landline try just [just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/pages/home.aspx)

- first call [just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/pages/home.aspx) access number (084 4720 0990 for landlines in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~1p
- first call [just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/pages/home.aspx) access number (084 4904 0990 for mobiles in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~4p

If you have a UK three mobile phone try [dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/)

- dial the [dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/) prefix (290429 for landlines in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~5p
- dial the [dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/) prefix (290729 for mobiles in Turkey) before your international number and pay ~8p

Note : These prefixs sometimes change so please check [just-dial.com](http://www.just-dial.com/pages/home.aspx) and [dialacode.com](http://www.dialacode.com/) sites for the latest access numbers and prefixs for your international destination.
