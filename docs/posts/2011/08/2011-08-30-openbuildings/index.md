[OpenBuildings](http://openbuildings.com/ "openbuildings") is a possibly the worlds biggest mashup of  [freebase](http://www.freebase.com/ "freebase") and [wikipedia](http://www.wikipedia.org/ "wikipedia") with the aim to _"collaboratively archive the world's built environment."_

It even features the Fizzy Bottle Roof Project !
