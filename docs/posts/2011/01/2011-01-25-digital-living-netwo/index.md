[Digital Living Network Alliance](http://www.dlna.org/home "dlna") (DLNA) is _a collaboration of the world’s leading consumer electronics, PC and mobile companies. DLNA has created design guidelines for a new generation of DLNA Certified products that can work together — no matter the brand._
