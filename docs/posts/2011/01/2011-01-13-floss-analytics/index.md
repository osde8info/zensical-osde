[Piwik](http://piwik.org/ "piwik") is a downloadable, open source (GPL licensed) real time web analytics software program.

_It provides you with detailed reports on your website visitors: the search engines and keywords they used, the language they speak, your popular pages etc etc_

- [http://piwik.org/docs/installation](http://piwik.org/docs/installation/)
- [http://demo.piwik.org/index.php](http://demo.piwik.org/index.php)
