Just started using the FLOSS PHP5 [Phoronix Test Suite](http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/ "phoronix") and its associated [Phoronix Live CD](http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=pts_desktop_live "phoronix") (for 64-bit CPUs only) for  performance benchmarking & performance testing PCs.

Now you can find out if the Intel T2390 or the Intel T9300 is the fastest ? And by how much ? Go to [http://global.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=search](http://global.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=search "phoronix") and enter T2390 and T9300 into the processor search box and find a testset that has been run on all/both the CPUs you want to compare.

For example here's the BYTE UNIX benchmark for a [Intel PII](http://global.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=profile&u=osde8info-25219-9163-9165 "phoronix") v [AMD Athlon](http://global.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=profile&u=anon-29015-6142-22566 "phoronix") v [Intel Core i7](http://global.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=profile&u=ptslive-18234-27603-10654 "phoronix").

See also [http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=documentation](http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/?k=documentation "phoronix")
