UK PostCode data is finally "free" and "open" and available as "Code-Point Open" which is _a postal geography dataset_ subject to the  [UK OGL licence](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/open-government-licence.htm "open gov lic")

- [data.gov.uk/dataset/os-code-point-open](http://data.gov.uk/dataset/os-code-point-open)
- [ordnancesurvey.co.uk/opendatadownload/products.html](https://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/opendatadownload/products.html)
- [ordnancesurvey.co.uk/oswebsite/opendata/](http://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/oswebsite/opendata/)
- [data.gov.uk/apps/mapit](http://data.gov.uk/apps/mapit)
