[DynDNS](http://www.dyndns.com/services/dns/dyndns/ "dyndns") allows you turn your ADSL ISP provided dynamic IP address  _into a  hostname that points to your home or office IP address, providing an easy-to-remember URL for quick access. They also provide an update mechanism which makes the hostname work with your dynamic IP address._

And the [OpenDNS](http://www.opendns.com/ "opendns") service [DNS-O-Matic](https://www.dnsomatic.com/) can be used to update [DynDNS](http://www.dyndns.com/services/dns/dyndns/ "dyndns") !
