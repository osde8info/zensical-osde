[Edubuntu](http://edubuntu.org/download "edubuntu") [10.10](http://edubuntu.org/news/10.10-release "edubuntu 10.10") is now available in 4 flavours

- ubuntu-edu-preschool - preschool
- ubuntu-edu-primary - primary
- ubuntu-edu-secondary - secondary
- ubuntu-edu-tertiary - tertiary

You can also install all [Edubuntu packages](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Edubuntu/AppGuide "edubuntu packages"), including artwork by installing the edubuntu-desktop package.
