[Acquia Drupal Commons](http://acquia.com/products-services/drupal-commons "acquia drupal commons") _gives community members many different ways to communicate, gives community managers ways to grow your community, and gives site owners plenty of options on how to set up your community site. Commons utilizes dozens of Drupal plug-ins, and sets up numerous Drupal functional elements for you in order to take the guesswork out of building a community site._
