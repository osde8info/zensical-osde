There are now three [android apps](https://market.android.com/ "android market") you can use with and [Ubuntu One](https://one.ubuntu.com/dashboard/ "ubuntu one").

- [Ubuntu One Files](https://market.android.com/details?id=com.ubuntuone.android.files "ubuntu one") that lets you see your [one.ubuntu.com/files](https://one.ubuntu.com/files/)
- [Ubuntu One Music](https://market.android.com/details?id=net.sourceforge.subsonic.u1m "ubuntu one") that lets you stream your music from your [one.ubuntu.com/files](https://one.ubuntu.com/files/)
- [TomDroid](https://launchpad.net/tomdroid "tomdroid") that lets you see your [one.ubuntu.com/notes](https://one.ubuntu.com/notes/)
