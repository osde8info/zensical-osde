howto create your own film company

- make some HD films / videos on your camera or mobile phone
- create some DVDs on [createspace](https://www.createspace.com/Products/DVD/ "createspace")
- add your films to [Amazon Video on Demand](https://www.createspace.com/Products/VideoDownload/ "amazon createspace vod")
- self-distribute and self-promote your films on [withoutabox](https://www.withoutabox.com/ "withoutabox")
