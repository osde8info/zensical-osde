if you ever forget which goog account you bought some media on click on the following goog play my library links and switch between each and every one of your goog accounts

- apps https://play.google.com/store/apps
- books https://play.google.com/books
- films/movies https://play.google.com/movies
- music https://play.google.com/music/listen?u=0#/artists
- newspapers https://play.google.com/newsstand/web/my/library

if you still cant find anything check your wishlist

- https://play.google.com/wishlist

if you cant find your android just goto find my android

- https://www.google.com/android/devicemanager?u=0
- https://www.google.com/android/devicemanager?u=1
- https://www.google.com/android/devicemanager?u=2
