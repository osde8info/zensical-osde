how to check your [octopus energy](https://share.octopus.energy/quiet-cloud-437) economy7 smartmeter switch is working

_all [octopus energy](https://share.octopus.energy/quiet-cloud-437)_ _tariffs feature 100% renewable electricity and no exit fees_

- sign up to [octopus energy](https://share.octopus.energy/quiet-cloud-437)
- login to your account
- look at your usage graphs
- check your storage heaters are using power from 00:00 to 07:00

before eco7 enabled heaters start using energy all day or start at 07:00 and stop at 24:00
