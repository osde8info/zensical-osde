Lloyds Bank plc (_"the UK's leading digital bank"_) announce their open source strategy - proof that FLOSS really is now mainstream if you needed it

- [Open Source Mission - Lloyds Banking Group PLC](https://www.lloydsbankinggroup.com/insights/open-source-mission.html?utm_source=social&utm_medium=twitter&utm_campaign=opensource&utm_term=paid)

> We believe that a robust open source strategy can support our purpose of Helping Britain Prosper, and that by proactively engaging with developer communities we can encourage inclusivity.
> 
> “Be open and use open source” is the motto of the UK’s government digital services internal guidance on open source software. It is good practice to publish your code, and to use open source to improve transparency, flexibility and accountability.
