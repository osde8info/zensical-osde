using @microsoftuk windows 10,  @google takeout and movie maker 10 to download, rotate and re-upload your @youtube videos #windows10 #youtube #video #download

windows 10 has a video editing app confusing called "Photos" but the rotate button is disabled when you are editing videos

![winphotvidnorot](https://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/winphotvidnorot.png)

google youtube does NOT let you download videos unless you pay for youtube premium ( or they are your own)

- [How To Download Other Peoples YouTube Videos By Paying For Premium](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IVPriyYwd-E)

you can use google takeout to download all you youtube videos and separate meta info in JSON files

- https://takeout.google.com/settings/takeout/custom/youtube
- https://takeout.google.com/settings/takeout/downloads?pli=1

you can also use youtube studio to download your youtube videos one by one

- https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/56100?hl=en

but

- _you can only download 1 video at a time (5 videos in 24 hours)_

once you have downloaded your videos you can download and run movie maker 10

download movie maker 10

- [movie maker 10](https://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/p/movie-maker-10-tell-your-story/9mvfq4lmz6c9?activetab=pivot:overviewtab)

run movie maker 10

- click rotate video button,
- choose your video,
- choose your rotation then
- save it

![movmak](https://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/movmak.png)

you can now re-upload the rotated version to youtube

![rotated](https://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/rotated.png)
