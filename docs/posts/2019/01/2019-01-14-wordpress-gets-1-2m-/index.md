_Through WordPress.com and our enterprise service [WordPress.com VIP](https://vip.wordpress.com/), we’re proud to host sites for some of the most trusted names in journalism — from [Time.com](http://time.com/) and CNN to [FiveThirtyEight](https://fivethirtyeight.com/) and [Quartz](https://qz.com/)_

_Today we’re excited to announce funding for a new platform, [Newspack by WordPress.com](http://newspack.blog), aimed at small- and medium-sized news organizations. Google, through the [Google News Initiative](https://newsinitiative.withgoogle.com/), is taking the lead in backing the project and has committed $1.2 million._
