turn your cinema into #yourcinema with help from @ourscreen crowdsourced cinema platform

https://www.ourscreen.com/about-ourscreen/

_ourscreen allows you to [host a screening of your favourite film at your local cinema.](https://www.ourscreen.com/create-screening/film/) Pick a film, [select a cinema](https://www.ourscreen.com/search/cinemas/), choose a date and time and away you go. Screenings can be exclusively for you and your friends or for anybody who wants to come. If enough people book tickets, the screening takes place_

https://www.ourscreen.com/how-it-works/
