debit card and credit card payment protection

some debit cards have "chargeback protection" which protects the amount you paid via your debit card

- https://www.money.co.uk/current-accounts/is-debit-card-protection-the-same-as-for-credit-cards.htm
- https://www.moneysavingexpert.com/shopping/visa-mastercard-chargeback/

credit cards have "section 75" protection which covers full amount of goods even if you only paid a percentage via your credit card

- https://www.money.co.uk/credit-cards/how-does-section-75-protect-your-credit-card-spending.htm
- https://www.moneysavingexpert.com/shopping/section75-protect-your-purchases/
