howto protect yourself from firesheep public wi-fi cookie attacks

**users** can add one of these plugins to their favorite browser

- [https://www.eff.org/https-everywhere](https://www.eff.org/https-everywhere)
- [https://github.com/nicksieger/sheepsafe](https://github.com/nicksieger/sheepsafe)
- [http://code.google.com/p/kbsslenforcer/](http://code.google.com/p/kbsslenforcer/)

**webdevs** & **webmasters** can use htaccess to redirect all users to the https version of your site

- [http://fsse.info/HtAccessSecure](http://fsse.info/HtAccessSecure)
