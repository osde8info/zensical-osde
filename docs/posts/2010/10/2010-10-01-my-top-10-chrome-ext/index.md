**Alexa Traffic Rank - Version: 1.0.9**

The Official Alexa Traffic Rank Extension, providing Alexa Traffic Rank and site Information when clicked.

**Amazon Wish List - Version: 1.0.0.1**

Add products from any website to your Amazon Wish List

**ChromeAccess - Version: 1.5**

Quick access to all (relevant) 'about:' and 'chrome://' pages.

**Delicious Bookmarks Extension (Beta) - Version: 0.998**

Delicious Bookmarks is the official Chrome extension for Delicious, the world's leading social bookmarking service.

**Firebug Lite for Google Chrome - Version: 1.3.1.7759**

Firebug Lite for Google Chrome, supported by the Firebug Working Group.

**Screen Capture (by Google) - Version: 2.0.2**

Capture visible content of a tab, a region of a web page, or the whole page as a PNG image. Support horizontal and vertical scroll when capturing whole page, with an all new autosave capability.

**Speed Tracer (by Google) - Version: 0.18**

Get insight into the performance of your web applications.

**Web Developer - Version: 0.3.1**

Adds a toolbar button with various web developer tools. The official port of the Web Developer extension for Firefox.
