My SAMSUNG Galaxy Portal GT-I5700 has just completely hung because i loaded too many programs and ran out of internal memory !

But thanks to the web i've found out how to  reboot / factory reset:

[http://forumuk.samsungmobile.com/PostsList.aspx?ThreadID=216345](http://forumuk.samsungmobile.com/PostsList.aspx?ThreadID=216345)

says

- press VOL DOWN + CALL BEGIN + CALL END
- wait for power on
- let go of press CALL BEGIN+ CALL END
- wait for strange ! ICON !
- press ^MENU^ button

See also

[http://www.samsung.com/uk/support/search/&searchWord=GTI7500](http://www.samsung.com/uk/support/search/supportSearchModelResult.do?menu=SP00&searchWord=GTI7500&default_searchInputBox_value=Enter+model+number+or+keywords&searchInputBox=GT-I7500&find_button=)
