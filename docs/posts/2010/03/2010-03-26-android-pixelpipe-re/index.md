If you ever want to upload a photo or video to dozens of [BMN](http://business-media-networks.groups.vox.com/)s and [SMN](http://social-media-networks.groups.vox.com/)s with a single click try [pixelpipe](http://pixelpipe.com/) on your android, iphone or symbian mobile! [pixelpipe](http://pixelpipe.com/) now supports dozens of [BMN](http://business-media-networks.groups.vox.com/)s and [SMN](http://social-media-networks.groups.vox.com/)s including flickr, photobox, photobucket and VOX !

[pixelpipe](http://pixelpipe.com/) will also save me a small fortune in mobile upload data costs since some UK 3G mobile networks charge between £1/M - £6/M (esp when roaming in europe).  
  
So uploading 10 x 1M photos will only cost me £10 instead of £80, £160 or even £320 if i were on 32 photo BMNs and SMNs !
