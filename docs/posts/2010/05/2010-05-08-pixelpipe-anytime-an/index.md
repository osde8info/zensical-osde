Not only does [pixelpipe](http://pixelpipe.com) let you upload photo(s) to [dozens of BMNs & SMNs](http://pixelpipe.com/capabilities) in a single click it is now available for hundreds of different devices !

- netbooks, notebooks and PCs (via [firefox plugin](http://pixelpipe.com/tools))
- [android](http://pixelpipe.com/tools), [iphone](http://pixelpipe.com/tools) AND [symbian](http://pixelpipe.com/tools) mobiles
- nokiaÂ [N900](http://pixelpipe.com/tools) mobiles  
    
- digital cameras that work with an [eye-fi SD card](http://blog.pixelpipe.com/2010/03/06/connecting-your-eye-fi-to-pixelpipe-for-social-web-publishing-awesomeness/) !

If you are a googlefan you can even connect it [indirectly to your googlebuzz](http://blog.pixelpipe.com/2010/02/10/getting-pixelpipe-plugged-into-your-google-buzz/) account and [use pixelpipe with photoshop.com mobile app](http://blog.pixelpipe.com/2010/04/12/editing-images-with-photoshop-with-in-pixelpipe-for-android/) on your googledroid !
