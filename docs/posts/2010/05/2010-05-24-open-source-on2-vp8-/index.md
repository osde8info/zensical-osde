Google have just released the ON2 VP8 Video Codec as Open Source and are now started to deploy it in the [Goog Chromium](http://blog.chromium.org/2010/05/webm-and-vp8-land-in-chromium.html) browser and on [Goog YouTube](http://www.youtube.com/html5) ! Adobe have already promised to include support for it in their latest [Flash Player](http://blogs.adobe.com/flashplatform/2010/05/adobe_support_for_vp8.html) !
