## FON WIFI CONNECTION MANAGER FOR NOKIA WIFI PHONES

#### Get connected easily with your Symbian device

The [FON WiFi Connection Manager](https://www.fon.com/en/download) for Symbian devices is a free tool developed by FON for Nokia WiFi phones users (Nokia E60, E61, N80, N91, N92, N93 and N95 models).  

  
All Foneros who own one of these phones are now able to connect them to thousands of FONspots.
