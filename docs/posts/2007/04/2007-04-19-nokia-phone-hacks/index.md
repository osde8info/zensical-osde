Here are a couple of useful NOKIA phone hacks:

- IMEI number \*#06#  
    
- Firmware version \*#0000#  
    
- WLAN MAC address \*#62209526#  
    

for example my Nokia firmware version is

- 1.0633.62.05
- 15-02-07
- RM-227
