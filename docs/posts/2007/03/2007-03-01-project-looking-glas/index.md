[Sun](https://lg3d.dev.java.net/) [Java Desktop](https://javadesktop.dev.java.net/) have just released [Project Looking Glass](https://lg3d.dev.java.net/) the ultimate 3D desktop!

It even has its own Looking Glass [blog](http://projectlookingglass3d.blogspot.com/).

Looking Glass [requirements](https://lg3d.dev.java.net/lg3d-getting-started.html#Requirements).

CPU      
1.4 GHz or faster recommended

RAM      
512MB recommended

Graphics Card      
3D accelerated graphics card, with at least 32MB VRAM and driver support for OpenGL, version 1.3 or greater (LG3D may run with OpenGL 1.2, but 1.3 is highly recommended). 24bit display depth is required in order for the X11 integration work correctly.

You can install Looking Glass on \*buntu or try the Looking Glass [Live-CD](https://lg3d-livecd.dev.java.net/Web-Site/Download.html).

Finally here are some Looking Glass [tutorials](https://lg3d.dev.java.net/tutorial/index.html).
