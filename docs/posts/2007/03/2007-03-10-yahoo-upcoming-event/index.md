Yahoo [Upcoming](http://upcoming.org/) is a community for discovering and sharing events. It can help you find stuff to do, discover what your friends are doing, or let you keep private events online for your own reference.

There is a brand new [Upcoming VOX group](http://upcoming.org/group/2798/) that you could join !

More [About Upcoming](http://upcoming.org/help/about/).

And here are some examples of [Upcoming](http://upcoming.org/) events in the UK  

- [The Future of Web Design](http://upcoming.org/event/135049/)
- [Social Media Club London](http://upcoming.org/event/162043/)
- [BarCampBrighton](http://upcoming.org/event/161582/)
- [PubStandards](http://upcoming.org/event/148676/)
