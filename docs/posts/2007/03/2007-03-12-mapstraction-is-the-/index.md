[Mapstraction](http://mapstraction.com/) is a JavaScript library which abstracts away the differences between online maps from major players (i.e. Google Maps API, YahooMaps API, MultiMap, Map24, [OpenStreetMap](http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/index.php/Main_Page) etc) and frees up developers from changes to terms and conditions, degradations in performance, addition of ads, etc:

- [RoadMap](http://mapstraction.com/trac/roadmap)
- [Wiki](http://mapstraction.com/trac/wiki)
- [Subversion Repository](http://www.mapstraction.com/svn/)
- [SWIK.NET](http://swik.net/mapstraction)  
    

  
Initial development of mapstraction was made possible with the generous support of [nestoria](http://www.nestoria.co.uk/).
