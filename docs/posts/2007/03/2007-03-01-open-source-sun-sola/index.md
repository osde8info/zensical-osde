The free and open source [Sun Solaris](http://www.sun.com/software/solaris/index.jsp) Operating System is available on hundreds of [x64/x86 platforms](http://www.sun.com/bigadmin/hcl) and supported for thousands of [open source](http://www.sun.com/software/solaris/freeware) and [ISV applications](http://www.sun.com/bigadmin/apps).

Sun say it "has the largest installed-base of any other commercial UNIX or Linux distribution on the planet."
