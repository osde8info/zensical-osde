[**Jokosher**](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jokosher) is a [free libre open](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_software "Free software"), [non-linear](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-linear_editing "Non-linear editing") [multi-track](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multitrack_recording "Multitrack recording") [audio editor](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audio_editing "Audio editing"), the [source code](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Source_code "Source code") of which is released under the [GNU GPL](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU_General_Public_License "GNU General Public License").

It is being developed in [Python](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Python_%28programming_language%29 "Python (programming language)"), using [GStreamer](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GStreamer "GStreamer") as an audio back-end, for the Linux operating system.

For more [free libre open](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_software "Free software") audio editing tools have a look at [swik.net](http://swik.net/?find=audio+editor)
