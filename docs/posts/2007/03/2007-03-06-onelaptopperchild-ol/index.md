[Sugar](http://wiki.laptop.org/go/Sugar) is the core of the OLPC Human Computer Interface.

![](http://wiki.laptop.org/go/Image:Sugar.png)

Sugar's goal is to turn the Laptop into a fun, easy to use, social experience that promotes sharing and learning.

You can download the [OLPC Sugar LIVE-CD](http://olpc.download.redhat.com/olpc/streams/development/build287/livecd/) from Red Hat or build [Building the XO: Introducing Sugar](http://www.redhatmagazine.com/2007/02/23/building-the-xo-introducing-sugar/) from Red Hat Mag.
