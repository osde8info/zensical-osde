del.icio.us [now](http://radar.oreilly.com/archives/2007/01/flickr_launches.html) let you "filetype" your tags [del.icio.us filetype tags](http://del.icio.us/help/mediafiletypes) as well as [make your audio links playable](http://del.icio.us/help/playtagger)  
and  
flickr now let you "geotag" your tags [flickr machine tags](http://www.flickr.com/groups/api/discuss/72157594497877875/) (see also [geobloggers](http://www.geobloggers.com/archives/))

tagging the tags !

will the semantics never end ?
