If you have a Google account just goto [Google Labs Reader](http://www.google.com/reader/view/) and just type "vox <someones-vox-name>" ie "vox osde-info" into the Google Reader search box and you'll instantly be able to subscribe to all their VOX RSS feeds ! Its a sort of [FeedBurner](http://www.feedburner.com/fb/a/home) in reverse !

You can even add Google Reader feeds to your Personalised Google homepage
