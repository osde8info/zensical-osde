Flickr advanced search [http://flickr.com/search/advanced/](http://flickr.com/search/advanced/)

has three check boxes

- Only search within Creative Commons licensed photos

> -      Find content to use commercially
> -      Find content to modify, adapt, or build upon

  
that generate 4 CC search variations

- CC any [http://flickr.com/search/?l=cc](http://flickr.com/search/?l=cc)
- CC commercial [http://flickr.com/search/?l=comm](http://flickr.com/search/?l=comm)
- CC derivative [http://flickr.com/search/?l=deriv](http://flickr.com/search/?l=deriv)
- CC commercial & derivative [http://flickr.com/search/?l=commderiv](http://flickr.com/search/?l=commderiv)

the later one is equivalent to CC-BY-SA
