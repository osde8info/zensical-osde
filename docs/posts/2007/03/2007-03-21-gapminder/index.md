[Gapminder](http://tools.google.com/gapminder/) is a non-profit venture developing information technology for provision of free statistics in new visual and animated ways.

Goal: enable you to make sense of the world by having fun with statistics.

Method: turn boring data into enjoyable interactive animations using [Flash](http://www.adobe.com/flash) technology.

Gapminder is a Foundation in Stockholm, Sweden. Funding has been mainly by grants from Swedish International Development Cooperation Agency, [Sida](http://www.sida.se/sida/jsp/sida.jsp?d=121&language=en_US).

In collaboration with [United Nations Statistic Division](http://unstats.un.org/) Gapminder promote free access to searchable public data and all animations of different types of data are freely available at [www.gapminder.org](http://www.gapminder.org/).
