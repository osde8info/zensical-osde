You are spoilt for choice (if you include free and non-free formats).

First there is a choice of [Multimedia Players](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MultimediaApplications):  

- gstreamer
- gxine
- helix
- mplayer
- totum
- xine
- xmms

then there are the formats:  

- [FreeFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FreeFormats)
- [RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
- [RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs)

  
and finally codecs:  

- [Codecs](https://help.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/codecs.html)
