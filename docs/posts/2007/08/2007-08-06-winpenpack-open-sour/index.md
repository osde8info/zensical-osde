[**WinPenPack**](http://www.winpenpack.com/main/news.php) is a collection of free and open source portable programs optimized for use with a USB pen drive and also directly from a folder of your Hard Disk. You can choose between some standardised configurations, all small enough to fit on your USB Pen Drive.
