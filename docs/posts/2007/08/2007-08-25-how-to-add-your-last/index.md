If you are an upcoming unsigned artist or musician and haven't already added your last.fm link then you can goto [http://www.vox.com/account/profile/](http://www.vox.com/account/profile/) and scroll down to the very end of the page

My Vox | Edit Profile | Additional Links

# List your personal websites and other blogs on your profile. Get more info.

  
THEN Just add [your last.fm page](http://www.last.fm/) to the list and then it's really easy for your fans to add your music to [their last.fm favorites](http://www.last.fm/user/osde8info/) ! And saves any potential fans from bugging you with emails asked what [your last.fm page](http://www.last.fm/) is !
