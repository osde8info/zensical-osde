if you have two flickr account and want to swap your flickr account yahoo email associations you'll need to create a third yahoo email account !

1. create NEW yahoo email addr
2. first flickr -> NEW email
3. second flicker -> first email
4. first flickr -> second email
5. delete NEW yahoo email addr
