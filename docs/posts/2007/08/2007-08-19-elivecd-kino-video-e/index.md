[KINO](http://www.kinodv.org/) is an open source video editor than runs on GNU/Linux distros such as Ed/X/Ubuntu !

[ELIVECD](http://www.elivecd.org/gb/Main/About/) is a multi-media Debian distro that already includes KINO or just download [KINO](http://www.kinodv.org/article/static/1) itself !
