TESCO VOIP SIP ATA lets you change your CODEC and view DIALPLAN

CODEC

  

[![Tesco VOIP ATA](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d09e76cb0dbe2b.png?w=300 "Tesco VOIP ATA")](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d09e76cb0dbe2b.png)

[Tesco VOIP ATA](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d09e76cb0dbe2b.png "Tesco VOIP ATA")

  

  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
your choices are  

- Dialup (use G.729/GSM-FR)
- DSL/Cable (use G.729/GSM-FR/G.726)
- LAN (use G.711/G.729/GSM-FR/G.726)  
      
    

  
DIALPLAN  
  

[![Tescodialplan](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d4144098e96a47.png?w=300 "Tescodialplan")](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d4144098e96a47.png)

[Tescodialplan](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/06/6a00d4141b9517685e00d4144098e96a47.png "Tescodialplan")

  

  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
  
default dial plan is  

- "(\*123|\*\*\*|\*xxxxxxxx|999|112|18000|00\[1-9\]x.|0\[1237\]xxxxxxxxx|01xxxxxxxx|x.|\*\*3)"
