[CivicSpace](http://civicspacelabs.org/) is a Drupal based CMS for civic groups

_Empower even the smallest civic groups to effectively deploy sophisticated fundraising, communication and advocacy technologies._

_CivicSpace On Demand is the easy way to bring individuals and groups together on the Internet._

- _Promote your cause and communicate with supporters via your website and e-newsletters._
- _Create and update your website content with no programing skills required._
- _Offer opportunities to support your cause by making online donations, volunteering or attending an event._
- _Enable people to interact, with powerful community-building tools like blogs, discussion forums, voting and petitions._
- _Keep track of all your contacts and your interactions with them in a powerful, integrated central database._
