## [EASTER EGG: Flight Simulator Hidden Within Google Earth](http://mashable.com/2007/08/31/google-earth-flight-simulator/ "Permalink to EASTER EGG: Flight Simulator Hidden Within Google Earth")

Amazing but true: as spotted by [Marco](http://marco-za.blogspot.com/2007/08/google-earth-flight-simulator.html), Google has hidden a mini flight simulator within [Google Earth](http://earth.google.com/). To access it for the first time, hit CTRL+Alt+A in Windows or Command+Option+A on the Mac.
