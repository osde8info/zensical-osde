Just add "+extraname" after your usual googlemail address:

- mygoogemailname+xxx@googlemail.com
- mygoogemailname+yyy@googlemail.com
- mygoogemailname+zzz@googlemail.com

email sent to any of the above email addresses will all arrive in your mygoogemailname email inbox !
