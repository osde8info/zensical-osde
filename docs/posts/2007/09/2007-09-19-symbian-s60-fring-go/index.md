Symbian S60 (eg NOKIA E61i) + [fring](http://www.fring.com/) + GoogleTalk + MSN + Skype + SIP (via [Euteliavoip](http://www.euteliavoip.com/index.php)) lets you communicate with everyone from everywhere (there's a Wi-Fi hotspot or GSM/EDGE/3G available) !

Yes ! fring works with all these protocols simultaneously including SIP ([RFC 3261 SIP](http://www.euteliavoip.com/index.php?option=com_wrapper&Itemid=54)) with the need for STUN !
