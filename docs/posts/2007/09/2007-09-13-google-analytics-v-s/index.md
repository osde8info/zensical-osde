Google Analytics v [StatCounter](http://www.statcounter.com/) on VOX

Its a simple choice Google Analytics uses JavaScript and doesn't work in your VOX homepage embed while [StatCounter](http://www.statcounter.com/) can just use HTML and does work in your VOX homepage embed !

[  
![unique visitor counter](http://c31.statcounter.com/NNNN/NNNN/NNNN/)  
](http://www.statcounter.com/)

  
[  
View My Stats  
](http://my.statcounter.com/project/standard/stats.php?project_id=NNNNNN&guest=1)

  
So now you can have webstats on your VOX page if you use [StatCounter](http://www.statcounter.com/) !

  

[![StatCounter](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/09/6a00d4141b9517685e00e398ab4c880002.png?w=300 "StatCounter")](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/09/6a00d4141b9517685e00e398ab4c880002.png)

[StatCounter](http://osde8info.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/09/6a00d4141b9517685e00e398ab4c880002.png "StatCounter")

  
To add GoStats or StatCounter to your VOX just click My VOX, Design, Customise My Sidebar, Embed, Customise then paste the counter HTML into embed section !  
  
Easy ?
