[Seagull](http://seagull.phpkitchen.com/) is an open source MVC based PHP framework for web development that pays you bounties for your [contributions](http://www.seagullproject.org/bounty/action/list/) !

The Seagull core is licensed under [BSD](http://opensource.org/licenses/bsd-license.php) and is free to use in Open Source and closed commercial projects. You must however maintain the original copyright notice as described in the license. The BSD license gives the enduser much greater freedom to use the software than any of the GPL family and its derivatives.

Claim your bounty with Seagull !
