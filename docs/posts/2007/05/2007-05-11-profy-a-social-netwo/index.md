[Profy.com](http://www.profy.com/) is a multi-author blog community where Web 2.0 innovation and news is presented to Profy  readers in an unbiased and insightful manner. Profy authors care about reporting on the latest social networking trends, technology advancements, Web 2.0 startups, breaking news and commentary. 

Profy use the term Web 2.0 as it applies to their vision of the perfect web. Profy see a web of interconnected people striving to do more, get more and enjoy the potential of this fascinating tool the Internet.  

Web 2.0 is not a buzz word for Profy, but a symbol for new.
