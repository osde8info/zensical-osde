Here are some differences according to the [Debian GNU/Linux 3.1 Bible](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Debian-GNU-Linux-Bible-Wiley/dp/0764576445/ref=sr_1_2/203-7996320-1135954?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1178098198&sr=8-2)  

- interactive browser  
    
- more flexible searching
- apt-get installs "depends" pkgs aptitude installs "recommended" pkgs as well
- aptitude removes automatic dependancies
