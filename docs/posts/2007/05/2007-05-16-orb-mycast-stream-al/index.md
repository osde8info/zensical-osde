Orb MyCast - stream all your digital media to any internet device

[http://www.orb.com/en/about  
](http://www.orb.com/en/about)Orb leverages the best in already available technology (hi-speed broadband, Wi-Fi, wireless internet access and sophisticated portable devices) to provide a brand new way for people to connect with their digital media in the manner "how, when and where" they most desire.  
  
[https://mycast.orb.com/orb/html/index.html#](https://mycast.orb.com/orb/html/index.html#)  
**Orb 2.0 is all about MyCasting - Be your own producer:**  
Create channels out of your music, photos, videos, and television.  
  
[http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006](https://mycast.orb.com/orb/html/index.html#)  
[Orb](http://wii.qj.net/tags/orb/967) Networks is priming itself to be a main contender against [Apple's](http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006#) [Apple TV](http://wii.qj.net/tags/apple-tv/12579 "formerly iTV, streams digital content to HDTV").

The company has just announced that their free [MyCasting](http://wii.qj.net/tags/mycasting/12615 "Orb Network service for streaming media") [software](http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006#) now works with the [Microsoft](http://wii.qj.net/tags/microsoft/94 "Xbox 360 Creator") Xbox 360, the [Nintendo Wii](http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006#), and the [Sony](http://wii.qj.net/tags/sony/880 "Father of PlayStation") PS3, giving gamers the ability to bring all digital media to their [TV](http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006#) screen without any additional costs or hardware requirements.

The free MyCasting software allows users to enjoy all digital media from their home [PC](http://wii.qj.net/Orb-s-MyCasting-does-Apple-TV-for-17-million-gamers-worldwide/pg/49/aid/87006#), as well as online videos, on any networked device with an internet browser - be it other PCs, laptops, PDAs, mobile phones with support for streaming players, and now, next gen videogame consoles.

[https://developer.orb.com/wiki/Main\_Page](https://developer.orb.com/wiki/Main_Page)  
Most Orb users enjoy all of its features by logging to [http://mycast.orb.com](http://mycast.orb.com/ "http://mycast.orb.com") and interacting with the web site directly.

In addition, Orb exposes most of its services via an application programming interface (API). This means developers and power users can now use the API to add Orb functionality directly into their existing or new applications, using their favorite programming languages.

Developers are encouraged to sign up for the Orb developer program and agree to our terms of use. Get started with the Orb API today!
