How do I access the new M$ BingChatBot?

_You can request access by selecting “Join the waiting list”. When you have cleared the waiting list, you’ll receive an email letting you know that you can access the new Bing at Bing.com – then you can start typing in your usual search box._

_The new Bing is also available in the chat experience, found at the top of search results._
