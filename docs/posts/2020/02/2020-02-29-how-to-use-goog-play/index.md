How to use goog play music to play CDs

- Config goog play music to use SD card
- Create a folder called PC / My Music and Android / SD / My Music
- Create a folder for each artist
- Create a folder for each album
- Rip CDs to your My Music / Artist / Album
- Connect android to PC
- Drag PC / My Music to Android / My Music
- Restart goog play music (it should auto detect new CDs)
