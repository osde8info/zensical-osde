[cloudflare.com/](http://www.cloudflare.com/) are offering a free of charge CDN ([cloudflare.com/plans](http://www.cloudflare.com/plans)) and reverse proxy web cache for web sites

- [cloudflare.com/features-cdn.html](https://www.cloudflare.com/features-cdn.html)
- [cloudflare.com/features-optimizer](https://www.cloudflare.com/features-optimizer)
- [cloudflare.com/features-security](https://www.cloudflare.com/features-security)
- [cloudflare.com/network-map.html](https://www.cloudflare.com/network-map.html)

theres even a CloudFlare plugin for WordPress

- [wordpress.org/extend/plugins/cloudflare](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/cloudflare/)

however be sure to take note of CloudFlare's T&Cs:

_CloudFlare may:_

- _Rewrite content in order to hide sensitive information from select visitors to your site._
- _Add cookies to your domain to track visitors, such as those who have successfully passed a CAPTCHA._
- _Add tracking codes or affiliate codes to links that do not previously have tracking or affiliate codes._
- _Add script to your pages to, for example, add services or perform additional performance tracking._
- _Intercept requests determined to be threats and present them with a block page._
- _Other changes to increase performance or security of your website._
