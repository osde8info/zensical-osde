[Zoho CRM for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.zoho.crm "ZohoCRM Android") offers _mobile access to your customer information available on the web at [http://crm.zoho.com](http://www.google.com/url?q=http://crm.zoho.com&usg=AFQjCNGpJRtXucdYF-M3rJGL-RQHHKrDZw). Zoho CRM offers you a complete customer relationship life-cycle management software for managing Sales, Marketing, Customer Support, and Inventory activities in a single system. If you don't have a Zoho account, you can easily create one by visiting [http://crm.zoho.com](http://www.google.com/url?q=http://crm.zoho.com&usg=AFQjCNGpJRtXucdYF-M3rJGL-RQHHKrDZw)_
