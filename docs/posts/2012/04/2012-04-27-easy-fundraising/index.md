you can now easily raise for for your favorite charity by shopping online or searching online

- [easyfundraising.org.uk](http://www.easyfundraising.org.uk/)
- [easysearch.org.uk](http://www.easysearch.org.uk/)

theres even a google chrome extension that automatically starts your donation from certain sites or redirects you to a pre-login page that will start your donation

- [easyfundraising.org.uk/find-and-remind](http://www.easyfundraising.org.uk/find-and-remind/)
- [chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/plfknkdmhngcjepkalkhgpmhpolandfp](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/plfknkdmhngcjepkalkhgpmhpolandfp/)
