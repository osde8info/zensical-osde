The correct FREDI T10 WiFi Camera Alarm Clock webcam factory reset procedure is to open the battery cover and press the tiny 1mm reset button for 10 seconds.

The printed instructions are WRONG and holding the MODE button for 6 seconds does NOT reset the webcam.
