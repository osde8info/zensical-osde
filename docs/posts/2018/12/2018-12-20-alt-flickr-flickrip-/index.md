only a few weeks until @smugmug kill @flickr #flickrip maybe its time to look at some alternative photo backup sharing sites but they all have drawbacks

- [500px](https://500px.com/osde8info) (no pngs)
- [everalbum](https://app.ever.com) (low res)
- [goog](https://photos.google.com/) (low res)
- [imgur](https://imgur.com/)
- [pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) (min size 4M)
- [shoebox](https://app.shoeboxapp.com/app) (low res)
- [yandex](https://yandex.ru/drive/)
