tips

do not link google drive to google photos unless you understand deleting implications

- https://support.google.com/drive/answer/6156103

_What happens when you change or delete a photo_

_If you edit photos in Google Photos, changes won’t show in Google Drive._ _When you delete a photo from Google Photos, it’ll also get deleted from Google Drive._ _If you delete a photo from Google Drive, it’ll also get deleted from Google Photos._ _But if you add that photo to an album in Google Photos, it won't be deleted from those albums._ _If you delete from the Google Photos folder in My Drive:_ _If you delete individual photos or videos, they will be removed from Google Photos._ _If you delete folders (including the Google Photos folder), photos and videos won’t be deleted in Google Photos. This is to avoid deleting all your photos and videos by accident._ _If you move photos from the Google Photos folder to a different folder and delete them from there, they’ll also be deleted in Google Photos._

_What happens when you delete a photo with Backup and Sync_

_If you delete a photo or video from Google Photos, it’ll also be deleted from Google Drive. If you delete from Google Drive, it’ll also be deleted from Google Photos._

_When you install, your setting will be set to “Don’t remove items everywhere,” but you can change it. Learn to change how your photos are deleted._

_Remove items everywhere_ _If you delete a photo or video from your computer, it’ll be removed from Google Photos and Google Drive._ _If you delete a photo in Google Photos or Google Drive, it’ll be deleted from your computer._ _Don’t remove items everywhere_ _If you delete a photo or video from Google Drive or Google Photos, it won’t be deleted from your computer._ _If you delete a photo or video from your computer, it won’t be deleted from Google Drive or Google Photos._ _Ask before removing items_ _When you delete a photo or video from Google Photos, Google Drive or computer, it will ask how you want to delete it._

_Note: It may take some time for your photos and videos to be deleted everywhere._
