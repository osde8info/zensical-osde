gatwick south and gatwick north airport passenger pickup for free / lowcost

goto long term (instead of short term !) and stay a max of 2 hrs for free

- _park in a designated bay, marked ‘Free Pick Up’, and wait in your vehicle for your passenger to arrive via free long term bus_
- or _enter as normal, park your car, and take the free bus into the terminal to meet your passenger as they arrive you can leave your vehicle here for free for two hours_
- https://www.gatwickparking.com/our-car-parks/passenger-collection/

BN, GU and RH residents can prepay £20 for 20mins for 250 pickups in short term

- https://www.gatwickairport.com/parking/other-parking-options/residents-discounted-parking-scheme/
